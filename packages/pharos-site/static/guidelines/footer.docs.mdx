## Usage

<div style={{ marginBottom: 'var(--pharos-spacing-3-x)' }}>
  <p>
    The footer contains links to various pages on the platform and information about copyright data.
    It is not sectioning content and does not introduce a new section in the outline of a page.
  </p>
</div>

<pharos-heading level={'2'} preset={'6'} style= {{color:'var(--pharos-color-text-base)'}}>Best Practices</pharos-heading>

<div style={{ marginBottom: 'var(--pharos-spacing-5-x)' }}>
  <div style={{ marginBottom: 'var(--pharos-spacing-3-x)' }}>
    <pharos-heading level={'3'} preset={'2'}>
      Dos
    </pharos-heading>
    <ul>
      <li>
        Use On Background, subtle links as these are best suited for navigational elements on
        darker, accessible backgrounds
      </li>
      <li>Ensure grouped links are related in the kinds of pages they link out to</li>
      <li>Use icons with links to social media pages</li>
      <li>Use text approved by Legal for the mission and copyright statements</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div>
    <pharos-heading level={'3'} preset={'2'}>
      Don'ts
    </pharos-heading>
    <ul>
      <li>Include links to specific content on the platform</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
